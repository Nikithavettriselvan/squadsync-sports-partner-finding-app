About Squad Sync

Squad Sync is a sports partner finder platform that helps players connect with teammates who share similar interests, skills, and availability. Whether you're searching for a badminton partner, finding cricket team members, or scheduling friendly matches — Squad Sync makes it simple and smooth.

Our goal is to help people discover new partners, improve their game, and build sporting connections with ease.
